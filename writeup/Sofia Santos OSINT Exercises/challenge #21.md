# TryHackMe OSINT Challenge Write-up  
## Identifying the Location from a Satellite Image of a Chocolate Bar

https://gralhix.com/list-of-osint-exercises/osint-exercise-021/

Date: Feb. 27,2026

### Task Briefing

The exercise required identifying the location depicted in a **satellite image of a chocolate bar**.  
The objective was to determine the **exact coordinates** using open-source intelligence methods, including keyword searches and image analysis.

Difficulty level: **Intermediate OSINT**

---

## Step 1 – Enumerate Important Keywords

Before starting the investigation, I identified key terms to guide the search:

- Maps  
- Chocolate  
- Chocolate bar  
- Satellite  

These keywords helped structure search queries and explore multiple avenues to find relevant sources.

---

## Step 2 – Define Pathways to the Answer

Several potential pathways were considered:

1. **Chocolate → Manufacturer site → Maps → Verify via satellite image**  
2. **Chocolate → Social media → Manufacturer site → Maps → Verify satellite image**  
3. **Satellite image → Google Maps / Google Earth → Match with known locations**  

These pathways were hypothetical guides to avoid tunnel vision and allow flexibility in investigation.

---

## Step 3 – Approach

Investigation relied on **two primary approaches**:

1. **Keyword Search** – Using combinations of enumerated keywords to locate manufacturer information and context for the image.  
2. **Image Processing** – Enhancing the image for better results in reverse image searches using tools like Google Lens.

**Important note:** If progress stalls, changing search perspectives or combining different keywords can prevent getting stuck in a “rabbit hole.”

---

## Step 4 – Image Analysis and Reverse Search

1. Processed the satellite image to improve visibility of landmarks and features.  
2. Submitted the processed image to **Google Lens**.  
3. Reverse image search revealed the chocolate brand: **PUCHERO**.  

---

## Step 5 – Verification

1. Visited the **PUCHERO website** to identify the source or location depicted.  
2. Cross-referenced with **Google Maps** satellite view.  
3. Matched the satellite imagery to confirm the exact location.

---

## Step 6 – Conclusion

By combining keyword search, social media exploration, image processing, and satellite verification:

- The chocolate was identified as **PUCHERO**.  
- The exact location of the satellite image was determined.

**Coordinates:** `41.352563, -4.690062`  

---

**Answer:**  
`41.352563,-4.690062`
