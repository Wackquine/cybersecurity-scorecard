# TryHackMe OSINT Challenge Write-up  
## Investigating Adam Scythe’s Artwork: “Involuntary Rat Queen”

https://gralhix.com/list-of-osint-exercises/osint-exercise-023/

Date: Feb 26,2025             

### Task Briefing

During the exercise, a **virtual machine video** reflected a desktop background at **3 minutes and 38 seconds**.  
The objective was to **identify the artwork and its artist**, as well as determine the potential cost of commissioning similar pieces.

Difficulty level: **Intermediate OSINT**

---

## Step 1 – Reverse Image Search

The desktop background image was captured from the video and submitted to a **reverse image search**.

Search results revealed the artist:

**Adam Scythe** – a digital and traditional artist known for surreal, fantasy-themed artworks.

The title of the piece was confirmed as:

**“Involuntary Rat Queen”**

This matched the visual content from the video and the image metadata found online.

---

## Step 2 – Researching the Artist

To understand more about the artwork and its value, the following steps were taken:

1. **Social Media Search:**  
   Searched for Adam Scythe’s profiles on platforms such as **Reddit** and **DeviantArt**.  
   Keywords used: `"Adam Scythe" + cost`, `"Adam Scythe" + commission`.

2. **Commission Costs:**  
   Posts on social media mentioned commissions but lacked precise pricing.  
   Further search on **DeviantArt** revealed:

   - Similar digital and traditional artworks by Adam Scythe start at **$190**.  
   - This aligns with the complexity and style of *Involuntary Rat Queen*.

---

## Step 3 – Analysis

Key observations:

1. The **desktop background** in the VM video corresponds to Adam Scythe’s work titled *Involuntary Rat Queen*.
2. The **artistic style** includes detailed character design, fantasy elements, and digital painting techniques.
3. Commissioning similar work would require a **minimum of $190**, depending on complexity and medium.

The exercise demonstrates the use of **OSINT techniques** to trace digital artwork and evaluate its potential cost.

---

## Conclusion

Through systematic analysis:

1. **Identified the artwork:** *Involuntary Rat Queen*  
2. **Confirmed the artist:** Adam Scythe  
3. **Estimated commission cost:** Minimum $190  

**Techniques applied:**

- Reverse image search
- Social media keyword search
- Cross-referencing DeviantArt posts for pricing

This exercise shows that publicly accessible sources can reveal both the origin and the estimated value of digital artworks.

---

**Answer:**

a) Artwork title: **Involuntary Rat Queen**  
b) Commission cost: **Minimum $190**
